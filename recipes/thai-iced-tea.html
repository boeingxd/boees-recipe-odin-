Thai iced tea

Description

If you're looking for a beverage to tame the spices of your Thai meal, this is it! Thai iced tea is a rich and creamy black tea sweetened with condensed milk and is the most popular Thai beverage.

Ingredients

  • Thai tea leaves
    The most popular brand is ChaTraMue.
  • Sweetened condensed milk
    Choose a brand with only a few Ingredients, such as Longevity brand.
  • Evaporated milk
    Evaporated milk is used to add creaminess.
  • Sugar
    You know, up to preferences.
  • Salt
    Just a little.
  • A lot of ice
    No ice, No Thai.
