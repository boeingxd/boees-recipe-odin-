Hainanese Chicken Rice

Description

My favorite Chicken rice dish, tasted in orchid road or whatever in Singapore, and damn it was the best Chicken rice ever.

Ingredients

Chicken:

  • Whole chicken
  • Table salt
  • Ground white pepper
  • Rich wine/strong>
  • Soy sauce
  • Knob ginger
  • Scallions
  • Pandan leaves
  • Toasted sesame oil

Rice:

  • Minced garlic
  • Minced fresh peeled ginger
  • Grain white rice
